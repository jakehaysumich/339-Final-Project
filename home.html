What is Country Swing?

Country Swing is a popular American partner dance which combines rapid-fire Swing moves with awe-inspiring dips and lifts and the anything goes attitude of Blues.

Country Swing is fast paced and combines well with many types of upbeat music. When paired with a slow dance such as two step, Country Swing can work with almost any song in any situation
