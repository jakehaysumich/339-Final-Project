Info

We have weekly meetings every thursday where instructors teach a lesson, usually a move sequence, then we break out into games followed by open swing dancing.

Typical meeting layout:

  • First 30-45 minutes break into basic and advanced lesson

  • Rejoin groups and play social dancing games: snowball, freeze dance, musical partners, etc

  • Handstand contest

  • Open Swing and advanced move/ariel practice

FAQ

  • Do I need a partner?

  • No, you do not need a partner to come, we can pair you up, but we do recommend bringing a partner to learn with

  • Do I need to know what I'm doing?

  • No, MSwing is no experience required and we teach a beginner lesson at every meeting that will help you get going

  • When do you meet?

  • We meet every thursday or wednesday depending on the week either 6-8 or 7-9 depending on avaliability

  • If I'm scared to dance with people better than me. Do I have to participate in dance games?

  • While no one ever has to dance with anyone, we strongly recommend dancing with more experienced dancers as thats how you get better

  • Where do you meet?

  • We meet in the Michigan League Room D on the third floor
